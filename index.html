I am a final-year PhD student in Computer Science at École Normale Supérieure under the supervision of Caterina Urban.

My research focuses of providing mathematical guarantees about programs behavior. My works explore the verification of temporal properties of sequential C-like programs. More specifically, I use Abstract Interpretation to develop static analyses proving CTL properties of program in adversarial context.
